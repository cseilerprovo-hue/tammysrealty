Why Utah County?

One of America's
Strongest Real Estate Markets

Utah County has experienced remarkable growth over the past decade — driven by a booming tech sector, two major universities, strong family formation rates, and one of the best qualities of life in the country.

Whether you're buying your first investment property or adding to an existing portfolio, the fundamentals here are strong.

  • BYU + UVU create strong consistent rental demand
  • Silicon Slopes tech corridor driving job growth
  • Strong population growth among young families
  • No state income tax on investment income
  • World-class outdoor recreation supports STR demand year-round
