Meet Tammy

Neighbor. Investor. REALTOR®.

Hi, I'm Tammy.
I live here too.

I'm a Mapleton resident, a wife, a mom of three, and a REALTOR® who genuinely loves what I do. Real estate isn't just my job — it's been part of my life for over 15 years.

Long before I got my license, I was buying, renovating, and managing properties across Utah County. I learned the market from the inside — what adds real value, what scares buyers away, and how to make a deal work for everyone. When I finally got my license through Relink Institute and joined Equity Real Estate Utah, it just made sense. I already knew this world. Now I get to help others navigate it.

I grew up in California's Bay Area, attended BYU (including a semester in Hawaii — highly recommend), and served an 18-month mission in Salt Lake City. Chris and I have built our life here in Mapleton and we're not going anywhere. This community is our community.

When I'm not helping clients buy or sell, you'll probably find me on a ski slope, a hiking trail, or a boat. I love interior design and writing, which — funny enough — turn out to be extremely useful skills for a REALTOR®.

"I'd love to guide you on your journey home — or through your next real estate investment. Let's talk."
